Hello! I am Miloni.

I am a data person, explorer and dancer.

Currently, I am a final-year student at BITS Pilani. Throughout my days at college, I have explored numerous things. But one of the most fascinating thing I discovered is the Power of Data. Through hands-on projects and courses based on statistics, natural language processing, image processing and neural networks, I have realised that making use of data is what I love. This led me to pursue internships at BNY Mellon, Walmart Global Tech, IIM Ahmedabad and CEERI Chennai, where I made use of different types of data to gain insights, detect anomalies and build products. In a nutshell, I really enjoy it when I get a raw dataset in hand and have to gain some useful insights or build something out of it.

I am intrigued whenever I discover something new and give my 100% to understand it (hence, I call myself an explorer). The most recent being User Interface. During the lockdown, I accidently started noticing the apps/websites that I use more carefully than ever before. This sent me spiraling into a vortex of articles and videos about design, typography and colours. This personal website is a result of what I learnt as a result of the curiosity. I have thoroughly enjoyed learning about user interface for the past few weeks.

If I am not doing either of these things, you'll find me doing my bit in making a change with Women of BITS or dancing. Scroll to find more!

Women of BITS

Over the three years at college, I came to the realisation that there are many female leaders across various fields but they are not being represented adequately. Moreover, I felt that there was a lack of awareness about opportunities at the right time among all students. Hence, we combined these two to build Women of BITS- an inter campus initiative aimed at increasing awareness about opportunities at the right time by showcasing success stories of BITSian women.
