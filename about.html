Women of BITS

Over my years at college I realised that opportunities are in abundance out there but most of the students miss out on them due to lack of awareness at the right time or lack of guidance. In BITS, many girls missed out on the numerous opportunities that companies offer just for girls due to the same reasons. This lead me and a few of my friends across the three BITS campuses to create Women of BITS. A platform where we showcase stories of BITSian women who carved a path for themselves in unique ways.
